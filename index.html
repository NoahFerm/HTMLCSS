Avatar

Information

Netflix's adaptation of Avatar: The Last Airbender(2005) made by Michael Dante DiMartino and Bryan Konietzko.

Siblings Katara and Sokka wake young Aang from a long hibernation and learn he's an Avatar, whose air-bending powers can defeat the evil Fire Nation

Release date: Presumably 2023

Cast:

  • Gordon Cormier as Avatar Aang
  • Ian Ousley as Sokka
  • Kiawentiio Tarbell as Katara
  • Dallas James Liu as Prince Zuko
